## Hypothesis

`skill_route_discovery` already maps external skill repositories into bounded
documentation, config, test, and code_patch lanes. The missing local invariant
was item-level provenance in the lane map: item-derived registries preserved
evidence URLs, but not selected digest `item_id` values. Carrying those ids into
registry entries and proposal lanes makes future proposal review more
replayable and less dependent on broad URLs.
