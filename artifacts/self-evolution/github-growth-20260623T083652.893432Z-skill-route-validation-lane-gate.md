## Hypothesis

An explicit `validation_lane_gate` in the local skill-route harness gives the operator a direct checkpoint for whether
documentation, config, test, and code_patch lanes have local replay commands, artifact proof, trust-boundary approval,
and supervisor readiness before promotion. This reduces ambiguity without widening activation.
